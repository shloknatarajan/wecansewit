Our Mission

We hope to alleviate the shortage of medical supplies across the United States by creating a streamlined donation pipeline from those who can make in-demand medical supplies (anyone, not just manufacturers) with those who need them the most. This doesn't include just hospitals, we also hope to provide for homeless shelters, nursing homes, and other care facilities. Let's keep our healthcare providers and at-risk populations safe, together.

How We Help

Makers

View comprehensive instructions on how to make critical medical supplies in short supply as well as how to distribute them to the nearest facility that needs them


Our most needed supplies are n95 mask covers (to extend the life of N95 masks), cloth masks, gowns, and 3D printed face shields. Instructions to make all of these available on our makers page.
